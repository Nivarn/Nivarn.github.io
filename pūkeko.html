Pūkeko

The Pūkeko chalet is a great option for larger families / groups that would like a private chalet to enjoy and explore the mountain range. The flexible adult to children ratio allows for atleast 3 families, with extra children slots for extended family. We have Large Bedrooms, A spacious warm shower, and a large dining area.

Maximum Adults : 6

Maximum Children: 15
