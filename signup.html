About Maunga Club

Your Own Dreamy Escape

The Maunga Club Offers a myriad of things when it comes to the biggest mountain range of New Zealand. The Maunga Club offers three different Chalets that you can reach by skiing/snowboarding to. Firstly, we have the Kererū Chalet, a great option for a Family Getaway. Secondly, we have our Pūkeko Chalet, an option for an escape for your large family / large friends group to enjoy. Lastly, we offer our mighty Kākāpo chalet, this is our largest chalet, including great amenities. p.s, bookings must be between Friday to Sunday.
